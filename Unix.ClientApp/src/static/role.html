Role Commands

Parameters are marked as optional if surrounded by ( ).

Some commands listed require you to have Administrator permissions within your guild's configuration, or be the guild owner.


role-add [role] - Adds the role to yourself. Fails if the role provided is not marked as self assignable.

role-remove [role] - Removes a role from yourself. Fails if the role provided is not marked as self assignable.

configure-role-add [role] - Marks the role provided as self assignable. Requires administrator.

configure-role-remove [role] - Unmarks a role as self assignable. Requires administrator.

role - Displays a helpful embed about this module.
