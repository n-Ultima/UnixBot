Guild Configuration Commands

Parameters are marked as optional if surrounded by ( ).

These commands require you to have Administrator permissions within your guild's configuration, or be the guild owner.


configure-modrole [role] - Configures your guild's moderator role.

configure-adminrole [role] - Configures your guild's admin role.

configure-requiredrole [role] - Configures the role that members must have to use commands. This only applies to non moderators and administrators.

configure-muterole [role] - Sets the mute role for your guild.

configure-messagelog [channel] - Sets the message log channel for your guild. Message edits and deletions are logged here.

configure-modlog [channel] - Sets the moderation log channel for your guild. Actions taken via commands with Unix are logged here.

configure-automod [true | false] - Sets whether to automoderate your server. True for yes, false for no.

configure-spam [amount] - Sets the amount of messages considered spam. If a user sends more than x amount of messages within 3 seconds, they will be warned.

configure-phisherman [api-key] - Sets the provided API key to your guild for use with Phisherman. View phisherman documentation to learn more.
