Automod Configuration Commands

Parameters are marked as optional if surrounded by ( ).

These commands require you to have Administrator permissions within your guild's configuration, or be the guild owner.


add-banned-term [term] - Adds the provided term to your guild's list of banned terms. If a user sends one of these terms in a message, they will be warned and said message will be deleted.

remove-banned-term [term] - Removes the provided term from your guild's list of banned terms.

add-whitelisted-guild [guildId] - Adds the guild ID provided to the whitelist, allowing invites pointing towards the guild through the automod.

remove-whitelisted-guild [guildId] - Removes the guild ID provided from the whitelist.
